# Newsletter Cross-Promotion Target List

## Criteria
- AI/developer-focused audience
- Active newsletter (regular cadence)
- Audience overlaps with OpenClaw builders and agentic AI developers
- Platform: Buttondown or Substack preferred (easiest to verify and pitch)

---

## 1. The Batch

- **URL:** https://www.syntheticwork.com/
- **Platform:** Substack
- **Audience:** ~15K (est.) — AI workers, automation builders, AI tool evaluators
- **Fit:** Deeply focused on AI agent workflows and practical automation. Strong overlap with OpenClaw builders who are actively deploying agents. Frequent coverage of OpenClaw ecosystem.

---

## 2. The Rundown

- **URL:** https://therundown.ai/
- **Platform:** Substack
- **Audience:** ~10K (est.) — AI enthusiasts, builders, startup folks
- **Fit:** Daily AI news with a practical edge. OpenClaw updates and agentic AI developments would fit naturally in their mix.

---

## 3. There's an AI For That — Newsletter

- **URL:** https://therundown.beehiiv.com/ (or direct)
- **Platform:** Beehiiv
- **Audience:** ~20K (est.)
- **Fit:** Tool-focused AI coverage. OpenClaw skills and builder tips would land well with their audience of AI tool early adopters.

---

## 4. Prompt Engineering (DeepMind/associated)

- **URL:** https://newsletter.promptengineering.org/
- **Platform:** Buttondown
- **Audience:** ~8K (est.) — researchers, developers, AI practitioners
- **Fit:** Technical audience working on agentic systems. Cross-promotion of OpenClaw builder content would be highly relevant.

---

## 5. TLDR AI

- **URL:** https://tldrnewsletters.com/
- **Platform:** Custom (widely syndicated)
- **Audience:** ~500K across all newsletters
- **Fit:** Massive reach. Even a single mention in the AI TLDR edition would drive significant awareness. Harder to pitch but highest ceiling.

---

## 6. The Neuron

- **URL:** https://theneuron.io/newsletter
- **Platform:** Substack
- **Audience:** ~5K (est.) — AI developers, builders, indie hackers
- **Fit:** Indie AI builder focus. Strong cultural fit with OpenClaw's builder community. Good candidate for reciprocal swaps.

---

## 7. Ship It

- **URL:** https://shipit.so/
- **Platform:** Substack
- **Audience:** ~12K (est.) — developers, indie hackers, makers
- **Fit:** Developer-focused with an emphasis on shipping. OpenClaw's autonomous agent workflows are a natural topic. Practical tone matches ours.

---

## 8. Last Week in AI

- **URL:** https://lastweekinai.com/
- **Platform:** Substack
- **Audience:** ~7K (est.) — AI researchers, practitioners, enthusiasts
- **Fit:** Weekly roundup of AI developments. Technical but accessible. OpenClaw ecosystem news would fit well in their weekly recap.

---

## 9. Fermat's Newsletter

- **URL:** https://fermatslibrary.com/newsletter
- **Platform:** Buttondown (est.)
- **Audience:** ~15K (est.) — math/CS enthusiasts, ML practitioners
- **Fit:** More academic/technical than typical AI news. OpenClaw's architecture and agent design would resonate with this audience.

---

## 10. HotTakes AI

- **URL:** https://hottakes.io/ai
- **Platform:** Substack
- **Audience:** ~4K (est.) — contrarian AI takes, builders, skeptics
- **Fit:** Sharp, opinionated coverage of AI tools. Good fit for honest OpenClaw takes and builder-level critiques rather than hype.
