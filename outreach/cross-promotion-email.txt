## Review Notes — Suggested Edits

### VARIANT A
- Cadence is **weekly** — keep that consistent everywhere you pitch.
- "~[X] subscribers" placeholder — either fill in a real number or remove the placeholder entirely. Sending a template with a visible "[X]" looks unpolished.

### VARIANT B
- "[topic — e.g., ...]" — fill in before sending. Use a specific article you can actually reference.
- "Would you be open to cross-linking?" — this is the strongest variant when you have a concrete piece to point to.

### VARIANT C
- Short and direct is good for busy newsletter operators. Keep this.
- "no cost" reads slightly more professional than "zero cost" for some audiences.

### General
- All variants should include the actual subscriber count once known. Placeholder "[X]" undermines credibility in outreach.
- Add a sentence about why you specifically value their newsletter (1–2 specific articles). Generic flattery is easy to spot; specific flattery works.
- Optional: PS with social proof (e.g., open rate) if you're comfortable sharing it.
