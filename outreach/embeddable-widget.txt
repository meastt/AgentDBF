# Embeddable Email Capture Widget — Plan

## Overview

Add a lightweight email capture form to the Agent Debrief site that posts directly to
Buttondown's embed subscribe endpoint. The form works without JavaScript (progressive
enhancement) and can be dropped into any HTML page.

## HTML Form Code

```html
<form
  action="https://buttondown.com/api/emails/embed-subscribe/agentdebrief"
  method="post"
  target="popupwindow"
  onsubmit="window.open('https://buttondown.com/agentdebrief', 'popupwindow')"
  class="subscribe-form"
>
  <input type="email" name="email" placeholder="Enter your email address..." required>
  <input type="hidden" value="1" name="embed">
  <button type="submit">Subscribe</button>
  <p class="privacy-note">No spam. One email per week. A new tab may open to confirm your subscription.</p>
</form>
```

## Placement Strategy

### 1. Homepage Hero (ALREADY IN PLACE)
The main index.html already has this form in the hero section. Keep it as-is.
URL: https://agentdebrief.com/

### 2. Blog Article Footers
Add the form at the bottom of each blog article, after the article content and before the
footer. This catches readers who finished an article and are most engaged.
CSS class: `.article-subscribe`
Target files:
  - /blog/what-is-agentic-ai.html
  - /blog/openclaw-getting-started.html

### 3. Lead Magnet Page
The /lead page already has a subscribe mechanism. Consider adding the embed form as a
secondary capture below the main lead CTA for visitors who don't convert on the primary CTA.
URL: https://agentdebrief.com/lead

### 4. Link-in-Bio Page
The new link-in-bio.html page serves as a social hub — add the embed form prominently at
the top for any visitor who lands there from a social profile link.
URL: https://agentdebrief.com/link-in-bio.html

## Brand Color Styling Notes

Use these CSS variables already defined in index.css:

  --bg-void:      #09090B   (page background)
  --bg-surface:   #18181B   (input field background)
  --border-color: #27272A   (input border)
  --text-primary: #FAFAFA   (input text, button text)
  --accent-cyan:  #00F0FF   (links, focus rings, accent highlights)

Form styling should match the existing `.subscribe-form` in index.css:
  - Input: bg-surface, border-color border, 6px radius, white text
  - Button: btn-primary (white bg, dark text)
  - On focus: border-color → accent-cyan (#00F0FF)
  - Privacy note: text-secondary color, 0.85rem font size
