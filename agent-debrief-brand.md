# Agent Debrief: Brand Guidelines

This document serves as the official brand guideline for the **Agent Debrief** OpenClaw agent. It outlines the core visual identity, including the color palette, typography, and logo usage, to ensure a consistent, minimalist, and authoritative presence.

## 🎨 Color Palette

Our color palette is designed to reflect a "Subtle & Confident" identity—tech-focused, clean, and highly readable. 

### Primary Colors

- **Deep Space Black (Background)**
  - **Hex:** `#0C0D11`
  - **Usage:** Primary background color for all interfaces, cards, and main structural elements. It provides a deep, immersive, and modern dark-mode aesthetic that reduces eye strain and makes accents pop.

- **Amber / Vibrant Orange (Primary Accent)**
  - **Hex:** `#E97929`
  - **Usage:** Primary accent color used for call-to-actions (CTAs), highlights, active states, and important graphical elements. It brings energy and focus to the minimalist dark background.

- **Cyan / Electric Blue (Secondary Accent)**
  - **Hex:** `#00F1FF`
  - **Usage:** Used for specific emphasis, status indicators, and highlighting key terminology (like "No Spam") to create a modern, high-tech interface feel.

- **Off-White / Cloud (Text)**
  - **Hex:** `#FAFAFA`
  - **Usage:** Primary text color for all headings and body copy against the dark background. It offers high contrast for readability while being slightly softer than pure white (`#FFFFFF`) to prevent absolute glare.

## ✒️ Typography & Aesthetic

- **Contrast & Hierarchy:** Rely on the stark contrast between the `#0C0D11` background and `#FAFAFA` text to establish a clear content hierarchy.
- **Accents:** Use `#E97929` sparingly to draw the user's eye to interactive elements or key data points in the agent's output.

## 🖼️ Logo & Iconography

- **Style:** Logos and icons should align with the "Subtle & Confident" framework—geometric, minimalist, and free of unnecessary clutter or emojis.
- **Coloring:** Logos should primarily utilize the `#FAFAFA` and `#E97929` colors against the `#0C0D11` background (or as transparent SVGs), ensuring they remain recognizable and sharp.

## 🤖 Agent Persona Alignment

The branding visually reinforces the OpenClaw agent's persona:
- **Dark Background (`#0C0D11`):** Represents depth of knowledge, technical proficiency, and an authoritative ethos.
- **Bright Accent (`#E97929`):** Represents quick action, energy, and the surfacing of valuable insights.
- **Clean Text (`#FAFAFA`):** Represents clarity, transparency, and straightforward communication.
